Step 2. In Outlook, go to File > Options > Mail and click on "Signatures".

Step 3. Click "New" to create a new signature. Give the a signature name you can remember & Click "Ok".

How to install this Html Signature on outlook 365

Step 1. Copy your signature by clicking the above button.

Step 2. Login to your Office 365 mail account.

Step 3. On the top right of your screen, click the Settings icon, then in the search box below, type in "signature".

Select the email signature option that shows up.

Step 4.Paste your email signature into the email signature settings box by pressing Ctrl + V (Windows) or Command + V (Mac). Press OK at the top of the settings to save.

Step 5. Now that you are in your Email Signature Settings, you can paste your email signature.
